Review Mail Content

Check the sender's domain name and email to verify the authenticity of the email.
(e.g. public domains such as @google.com and @yahoo.com are considered unreliable)

Avoid Clicking

When receiving an email that you deem suspicious or possibly a phishing email, avoid clicking on any links or attached files to prevent downloading any unwanted or malicious content.

Use Extension Functionality

(Optional) Utilize the Website URL Checker functionality to verify if the website has been flagged as unsafe by Google.

Forward and Report to IT

If the email and its content is considered as malicious, forward the email to the IT Team for review and removal on the system when necessary.

Block and Report Mail

Finally click on Block "Sender Name" and proceed to Report Spam the email right after.
